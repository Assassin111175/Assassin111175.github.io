HOW TO COOK A STEAK IN 11 MINUTES

Published by Nicky Corbishely

ingredients
  • Start with a 2.5 cm thick ribeye or sirloin steak
  • Ribeye tends to have a chunk of fat in the middle
  • Sirloin has smaller veins of fat
    • Important to note-
  • Look out for gristle on the steak. It is the rubbery cartillage of the steak
  • Next it is time to cook the steak
  • High smoke oil like sunflower oil is needed
  • Plenty of salt and grounded black pepper
  • Some garlc, fresh thyme or rosemary springs, and of course a little butter
  • Coat steak on both sides with oil then make sure to add plenty of salt and pepper
  • Heat up your pan and add your steak to it
  • Cook for 4 minutes until you turn it
  • During the last minute add butter to pan, also a few lightly peeled garlic cloves, and some thyme
  • Take steak out of the pan after cooking and leave to rest for 4-5 minutes
    • Now you have a nice cooked stek for you and your family Enjoy(:
    Important things to consider

    The type of pan to use would be a heavy-based frying pan like a cast iron or a griddle pan

      Make sure to cook no more than 2 steaks at a time
      Important to only iol the steak and not the pan!
